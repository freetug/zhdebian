Older ViEmu versions

Starting with ViEmu 2.5, new releases require a valid support contract. You need a valid support contract at the release date of each new version for your license to work with it. Support renewals are valid for a year after they are purchased. Check your license, which you probably received by email, or which you can always check at the Symnum customer portal, for your support expiry date.

If you don't renew your support, you can still use your license for older versions of the ViEmu products. Here are the links to download those older versions, together with the date of support required for your license to be valid with them:
